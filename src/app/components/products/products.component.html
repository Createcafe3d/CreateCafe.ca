Learn how to 3D Print in the Café

Classes Day Pass A 1-Day print pass gives you access to unlimited printing and a free coffee in Create Cafe during operating hours. The possibilities are endless as to what you can print!

Before you can use a daily pass you must take the Introduction to 3D Printing course to learn to operate the printers. You only need to take it once! Monthly Membership Becoming a 3D Printing Member at Create Cafe gives you access to unlimited printing for one month in the cafe. The possibilities are endless as to what you can print!

Before you can use a daily pass you must take the Introduction to 3D Printing course to learn to operate the printers. You only need to take it once!
