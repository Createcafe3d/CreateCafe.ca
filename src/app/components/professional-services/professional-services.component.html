Create Cafe specializes in designing for printability. Developed from several years experience in design and 3D Printing the team has the ability to model mechanical parts and free form structures with high precision able to be replicated immediately.
3D Printing FDM/SLA
Create Cafe currently has 2 printing technologies in-house: FDM and SLA. Fused Disposition Modelling (FDM) can use a variety of thermoplastics such as ABS, PLA, Nylon, and TPU to produce 3D objects between 100 - 300 Microns of accuracy. Maximum build volume for these printers are 500mm X 500mm x 500mm (19"x 19"x 19").

Stereolithography (SLA) produces very detailed and accurate objects between 20 - 100 microns using a laser and liquid resin. There are a variety of resins avaliable to use for different applications. Maximum build volume is 150mm x 150mm x 150mm (6"x 6"x 6").
